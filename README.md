**Approach:** Hypothetical pitch to the Strategic Equity Capital fund of Gresham House. Micro-cap focus, Bottom-up approach to business fundamentals, identification of niche industry.

**Financial Modelling:** Constructed a three-statement model of SRT Marine Systems, and produced a Perpetual Growth Discounted Cash Flow Model to estimate the fair value of the stock. Base, Bull, and Bear cases were constructed, along with a Football Field sensitivity analysis of WACC & Perpetual Growth Rate Assumptions.
